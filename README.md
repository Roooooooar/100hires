# 100Hires Research Project

## Topic: AI-Powered SEO Content Production

This repository contains research into how top practitioners are using AI to research, write, optimize, and scale SEO content that ranks on Google and gets cited by LLMs.

## Why This Topic

AI-powered SEO content production is the intersection of two major shifts:
1. AI tools making content creation faster and cheaper
2. LLMs becoming the new search interface

### YouTube Transcripts
5 recent video transcripts each from:
- Nathan Gotch - Founder of Rankability, AI SEO platform
- Vasco Monteiro - Founder of Arvow, AI SEO content platform
- Edward Sturm - Enterprise SEO consultant (Microsoft, P&G, ADP)
- Matt Diggity - Data-driven SEO practitioner, 160K+ subscribers

### LinkedIn Posts
5 curated posts each from:
